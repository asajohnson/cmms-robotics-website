What We Do

Compete

We participate in FIRST LEGO League (FLL) competitions, tackling real-world challenges through robotics, research, and teamwork.

Learn

Students develop skills in engineering, programming, problem-solving, and presentation -- skills that last a lifetime.

Grow

Starting with five FLL teams, we're building toward adding FTC and expanding opportunities for even more students.

Why "Alloy"?

An alloy is multiple metals combined together for strength. Just like an alloy, we are made up of multiple FIRST Robotics teams -- each one unique, but stronger together. Located on Cougar Mountain in Issaquah, we chose the name Cougar Mountain Alloy Robotics Teams to reflect both where we come from and what we believe: that collaboration makes us all stronger.
